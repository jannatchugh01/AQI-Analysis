# 🌍 Air Purifier Market Research Using AQI Analytics

## 📌 Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Codebasics Resume Project Challenge**, where participants were tasked with conducting market research for a startup, **AirPure Innovations**, using AQI, health, vehicle, and population datasets.

The objective was to determine the market potential for air purifiers in India by analyzing air quality trends, health impacts, and demographic factors. Using SQL-based data analysis, this project identifies high-priority cities, key pollutants, and strategic recommendations for product development and market expansion.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

AirPure Innovations is exploring the development of air purifiers to address India's growing air pollution crisis. Before investing in production and R&D, the company needs to understand whether there is a sustainable market demand for its products.

This project analyzes Air Quality Index (AQI), health surveillance, vehicle registration, and population datasets to:

* Identify cities with persistent air quality challenges
* Understand the health impact of poor air quality
* Determine the pollutants that should be targeted
* Evaluate factors influencing air purifier demand
* Recommend product features and market expansion strategies

## 📊 Key Insights

* Several Indian cities continue to experience persistently poor AQI levels, indicating significant air quality challenges.
* States with higher AQI levels generally reported greater disease burdens, highlighting the health impact of pollution.
* EV adoption alone has not resulted in substantial AQI improvements in all regions, suggesting multiple contributing pollution sources.
* Large urban centers with high population density and poor air quality present the strongest market opportunities for air purifier adoption.
* PM2.5 and related pollutants emerged as critical targets for air purification solutions.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### Market Strategy

* Prioritize highly polluted cities for initial product launch.
* Focus marketing efforts on regions with high AQI and large populations.

### Product Development

* Target PM2.5, PM10, NO₂, SO₂, and VOC pollutants.
* Incorporate real-time AQI monitoring and smart connectivity features.
* Develop tiered pricing models to serve different customer segments.

### Growth Strategy

* Use AQI and health data to continuously identify emerging markets.
* Position products as health-protection solutions rather than lifestyle products.
